Asun

How to make Asun meat delicacies

Asun is a peppered goat meat in Western part of Nigeria

Ingredients

  • 2kg (2.6lbs) goat meat (cut with the skin)
  • 2 habanero peppers (or to your taste)
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 big stock cubes
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 cooking spoon vegetable oil
  • Black pepper (optional)
  • 1 small green bell pepper
  • 1 small red bell pepper

Steps or Methods of preparation

  1. Cut the goat meat into big chunks. Make sure you cut the chunks with the goat skin.
  2. Add the thyme and black pepper; rush and add the stock cubes. Feel free to add other spices and ingredients.
  3. Cover the bowl with a thin film. Put it in the fridge and leave to marinate for about an hour.
  4. After marinating the goat meat,put the meat in the pot.
  5. Pour water to just half of the level of the goat meat, add the big chunks of onions and start cooking at medium heat.
  6. Lay the goat meat flat on your oven rack.
  7. Grill/broil in the oven till the meat becomes brown all over. You will need to turn them from time to time
  8. When the meat is well grilled/broiled, heat the vegetable oil in a clean pot.
  9. Add the pepper and the thick stock from cooking the meat (if any) and fry for a bit.
  10. Add the grilled goat meat
  11. Stir very well till the pepper is even distributed on the goat meat. The vegetable oil ensures that this happens and gives the Asun a nice glow.
  12. Your Asun is ready
